Why Medical Spa Means Medical

Safety Is the Quiet Part of the Work.

The line between a spa and a medical practice is the standing physician, the sterile technique, and the protocol when something goes sideways. We have all three, in writing, on the wall.

Physician Oversight

A board-certified dermatologist reviews every chart before treatment and is on the floor or on call for the duration of the appointment. No exceptions, no remote-only days.

Sterile Technique

Single-use injectable trays, closed-system reconstitution, and chlorhexidine prep before any needle. Treatment rooms are turned over with the same checklist used in surgical clinics.

Hyaluronidase On Site

Vascular complication is the rare event that defines whether a practice is medical. We keep hyaluronidase stocked, dosed, and within arm's reach of every filler treatment, every visit.

Aftercare Direct Line

Every patient leaves with a written aftercare plan and the lead injector's direct line. Bruising, asymmetry, anything unusual, you reach a clinician within an hour, including weekends.
