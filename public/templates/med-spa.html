Meet the Physician

Dr. Elaine Whitfield, MD

Board-Certified Dermatologist · Founder, Reverie Aesthetics

I trained at Columbia and completed my residency at NYU Langone before bringing my practice to Dallas in 2010. I have spent fifteen years on the principle that aesthetic medicine should make people look like rested versions of themselves, not different versions of themselves.

Every injectable in this practice is performed by me personally. Not a nurse, not a rotating provider, not a physician assistant. Me. The person you consult with is the person who treats you. There are no exceptions and there are no upsells.

We see twelve patients a day, by intention. The slower pace means longer consultations, more thoughtful planning, and the ability to say no to treatments I do not believe will serve you. Restraint is the practice.

Treatments

What We Offer.

A focused menu of physician-administered treatments. Every protocol is tailored to the individual face, never to a template.

Botox & Dysport

Precision neuromodulator placement to soften expression lines without flattening movement. We dose conservatively and adjust at every follow-up.

From $14 / unit

Dermal Fillers

Hyaluronic acid restoration of cheek, lip, and tear-trough volume using a layered, anatomic approach. The aim is structure that reads as rest, not augmentation.

From $850 / syringe

Laser Skin Resurfacing

Fractional and pico-laser protocols for tone, texture, and pigment correction across all Fitzpatrick types. Recovery is measured in days, not weeks.

From $1,200 / session

Chemical Peels

Medical-grade peels formulated and layered in clinic for fine lines, melasma, and dullness. We program a series across seasons rather than a single dramatic event.

From $385 / treatment

Body Contouring

Non-surgical contouring with CoolSculpting Elite and Emsculpt Neo for stubborn, exercise-resistant areas. Best paired with a maintenance plan and realistic timelines.

From $750 / cycle

IV Therapy & Wellness

Physician-formulated infusions for hydration, immune support, and post-procedure recovery. Each protocol is reviewed against your labs and intake before administration.

From $225 / drip
