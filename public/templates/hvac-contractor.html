What Sets Us Apart From Every Other HVAC Company.

The HVAC industry has a reputation problem. We built this company to be the opposite of that reputation. Here's how.

✓ Flat-Rate Pricing

Every job priced before we start the work. The number you approve is the number on the invoice. No labor padding, no mystery fees, no "while we were in there" surprises. If the job goes long, that's our problem, not yours.

✓ Background-Checked Technicians

Every tech on staff is a W-2 employee, drug-tested, background-checked, NATE-certified, and uniformed. No subcontractors, no day labor. The person at your door has been with Ironclad an average of seven years.

✓ We Don't Upsell

If your 14-year-old unit has another summer in it, we'll tell you. If a $40 capacitor solves the problem, we replace the capacitor. Our techs work on salary plus tenure bonus, not commission. There is nothing to gain from selling you something you don't need.

✓ Texas Heat Specialists

We've been doing this in DFW since 2004. We know which neighborhoods have the worst attic insulation, which subdivisions Builder X cheaped out on duct sizing, and exactly how a 105 degree afternoon punishes an undersized condenser. Local knowledge saves you money.
