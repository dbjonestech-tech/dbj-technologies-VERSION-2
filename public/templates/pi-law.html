Practice Areas

What We Handle.

Auto and Trucking Collisions

Highway crashes involving commercial vehicles, 18-wheelers, and rideshare drivers. We work with accident reconstructionists, ECM data, and federal trucking regulations to build cases the carriers cannot dismiss as routine fender benders.

Medical Malpractice

Surgical errors, missed diagnoses, hospital negligence, and birth injury. These cases require specialized medical expert review and a willingness to litigate against well-funded defense firms. We do not refer them out.

Premises Liability

Serious falls, negligent security, and unsafe property conditions on commercial sites. We pursue both the immediate operator and the corporate owner, because liability often hides one entity removed from the scene.

Wrongful Death

Representing surviving spouses, parents, and children when negligence has taken a family member. We handle these cases with the discretion they deserve and the financial preparation they require to make defendants answer at trial.

Catastrophic Injury

Traumatic brain injuries, spinal cord damage, severe burns, and amputations that change a life permanently. We coordinate life-care planning experts to quantify a lifetime of medical, vocational, and personal loss accurately.

Insurance Bad Faith

When carriers refuse to honor coverage they sold in good faith, Texas law provides a separate cause of action. We pursue extra-contractual damages where insurers have delayed, denied, or undervalued legitimate claims.
