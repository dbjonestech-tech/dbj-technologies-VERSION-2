Inside the Office

Modern Technology in a Comfortable Setting.

The tools we chose change what is possible in a single visit. The amenities we chose change how the visit feels.

Technology

  • 3D iTero Digital Scans No goopy impressions. Aligner planning, crown fits, and treatment previews in minutes, not weeks.
  • CEREC Same-Day Crowns Designed, milled, and bonded in one appointment. No temporaries, no second visit, no second numbing.
  • Intraoral Cameras You see what we see on the chairside monitor. Treatment recommendations come with the photo of why.
  • Low-Dose Digital X-Ray About 90% less radiation than film. Images appear on screen the same second they are captured.
  • Sedation Suite Nitrous on every chair. Oral sedation by prescription. IV sedation by appointment with our anesthesiologist.

Comfort

  • Overhead Netflix & Spotify Pick your show or your playlist when you sit down. Headphones cancel the drill, your choice cancels the boredom.
  • Heated Massage Chairs Every operatory chair has a warming layer and a gentle massage setting. Both are optional, both are quiet.
  • Weighted Blankets & Pillows For anxious patients, the weighted lap blanket is a small thing that helps a lot. Ask the assistant when you arrive.
  • Pediatric Room Its own waiting area, its own books, its own playlist. Designed so siblings can be seen back to back without losing the calm.
  • Sterilization You Can See Our instrument sterilization room is glass-walled. We follow CDC and OSHA standards and we are happy to walk you through the room.
