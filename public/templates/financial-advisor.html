My Fiduciary Pledge

I am a fiduciary. I do not take commissions. My only revenue is from clients.

Beckett Wealth Partners is a fee-only registered investment advisor. I am paid by the families I serve, and only by them. I accept no commissions, no soft-dollar arrangements, no revenue-sharing from fund companies, no insurance overrides, and no referral fees from outside professionals.

Where Your Assets Are Held

Your Money Stays in Your Name, at a Major Custodian.

You do not write a check to Beckett Wealth Partners. You open accounts in your own name at one of the institutional custodians below. We are granted limited trading and reporting authority on those accounts. Nothing else.

That structural separation is the single most important fraud-resistance signal a registered investment advisor can offer. You can log in to your custodian directly, twenty-four hours a day, see every position and every fee, and verify our work against an independent record of truth.

Trust by Construction

How I Protect Client Data.

The structural defenses below are not a marketing list. They are the operating practices a regulated advisor is expected to maintain, plus a few I have added because the threat surface keeps changing.

Custodial Separation

Client assets are held at Schwab, Fidelity, or Pershing in your name. The firm has limited trading authority only. We never accept incoming wires from clients.

Encrypted Document Vault

All document exchange happens inside the eMoney secure vault with multi-factor authentication. Tax returns, statements, and estate documents never travel by unencrypted email.

Annual Penetration Testing

Independent third-party security review and penetration testing performed annually. Internal phishing simulation and staff cybersecurity training run quarterly. Incident response plan reviewed yearly.
