Our Philosophy

We Sit on Your Side of the Table.

Beckett Wealth Partners is structured as a fee-only registered investment advisor. We are paid by you, and only by you. We accept no commissions, no soft-dollar arrangements, no revenue-sharing from fund companies, and no referral fees from outside professionals.

That structure removes the conflicts of interest that quietly shape advice at the wirehouses and broker-dealers. It is the simplest way we know to ensure that the recommendation you receive is the recommendation we believe is right for you.

Fee-Only

One transparent advisory fee, billed quarterly against assets under management. No commissions. No hidden product compensation. No surprises.

Fiduciary

We are legally bound to act in your best interest at all times. Not a suitability standard. A fiduciary one, in writing, on every recommendation.

Independent

Privately held, owner-operated, and beholden to no parent bank, insurance carrier, or product platform. The advice you receive is ours alone.
