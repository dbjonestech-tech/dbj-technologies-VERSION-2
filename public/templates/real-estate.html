MEET LAUREN

Real Estate at the Highest Level Is Not About Houses.

It is about reading a room before anyone speaks. It is about understanding what a family is actually trying to build, and which of two beautiful properties will quietly hold that life better. After eighteen years and more than two hundred transactions, that is the work I have learned to do well.

I spent the first decade of my career studying Park Cities one block at a time. I know which streets keep their light through the afternoon, which builders age with grace, and which homes are owned by people who will never list publicly but might, given the right call. I think about real estate the way a good interior designer thinks about a room, in proportion, restraint, and the quiet logic of how a life actually unfolds inside walls.

My practice is intentionally small. I take a limited number of clients each year so that every introduction, negotiation, and closing receives my direct attention. That is the only way I know how to do this honestly.

NEIGHBORHOOD EXPERTISE

I Know These Streets Because I Live on Them.

Highland Park

MEDIAN $3.4M · 28 TRANSACTIONS YTD

The standard against which Dallas measures itself. Quiet streets, deeply established families, and an inventory that turns mostly off-market. Knowing who is preparing to sell, often a year before the listing, is the entire game here.

University Park

MEDIAN $2.1M · 41 TRANSACTIONS YTD

Family-anchored, walkable, and shaped around the university. Buyers care about block-by-block character, school feeders, and the calm rhythm of neighbors who have known each other for decades. I match clients to streets, not just houses.

Preston Hollow

MEDIAN $4.2M · 22 TRANSACTIONS YTD

Larger lots, longer setbacks, and a culture of privacy that prefers a quiet phone call to a marketing campaign. Here the work is mostly intuition and relationships. I represent both ends of the table with equal care.

Prosper & Southlake

MEDIAN $2.8M · 34 TRANSACTIONS YTD

Newer estates, larger acreage, and clients arriving from the coasts who want space and stillness without leaving Dallas’s orbit. The buyer here is decisive, exacting, and rewards an agent who has actually walked the land.
