# Business Strategy

## Positioning

DBJ Technologies is a digital growth consulting and implementation studio, not an agency. The model: Pathlight diagnoses. DBJ fixes. Businesses grow.

The "Anti-Agency Model" means: one experienced architect, no overhead, no junior devs, no template sites. Full source code ownership, no lock-in, no dependency on DBJ to keep the lights on after the project ships.

## Target Market

Small-to-mid-sized businesses in the DFW metroplex (Dallas, Fort Worth, Plano, Frisco, Richardson, Arlington, McKinney, Denton corridor). Service businesses where one web lead equals real money: mechanic shops, law firms, dental practices, HVAC contractors, restaurants, real estate agents, construction companies.

## Sales Funnel

Pathlight is the spear. The funnel: free scan -> scored report with revenue impact -> email nurture (4 emails) -> Calendly booking -> discovery call -> DBJ project proposal.

Tyler (Soil Depot owner) is the primary warm referral source. He has been texted and told the site is live. LinkedIn company page is active for passive credibility.

## Revenue Model

Project-based engagements ($4,500-$25,000+) with optional $299/month maintenance plans. Pathlight operates at near-zero marginal cost as a lead generation tool. Eventually, Pathlight may become a standalone SaaS product with its own pricing tiers.

## Template Strategy

Star Auto Service is the first reusable template foundation for mechanic shops. Future templates planned for restaurants, law firms, dental, trades contractors. Each template reduces per-project labor while maintaining custom positioning.

## Competitive Advantage

AI-integrated workflow means faster delivery, deeper analysis, and architecture that would cost 3-5x at a traditional agency. Pathlight as a diagnostic tool is genuinely novel: the combination of vision AI + structured scoring + revenue modeling + conversational agent + automated nurture does not exist as an integrated product anywhere in the market.

## Brand Voice

First person ("I"), not "we." Direct, confident, detail-obsessed. Hospitality mindset applied to software engineering. White glove service. Always available for clients. No em dashes in any copy.

## What DBJ Says No To

Pornography and similar content. Anything that conflicts with the brand's integrity.
